# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Property owners and small businesses in and around Düsseldorf looking for a painter/plasterer who does more than flat-color walls — specifically creative wall design (Kalkputz, Marmorputz, Lasuren, individual color concepts). They're comparing local Handwerksbetriebe online before calling, and want visible proof of craft (real project photos) rather than marketing claims. They're evaluating trustworthiness and skill level, not shopping on price alone.

## Product Purpose

Convert visitors into phone calls or contact-form leads for Daniel Rudek's Malerfachbetrieb by presenting the craft (Kalkputz, Marmorputz, Lasuren, ecological materials, individual Farbkonzepte) as considered, premium work — not generic housepainting — while staying locally credible and trivially easy to contact.

## Brand Personality

Warm editorial craft / mineral interior atelier. Should read like an interior-design magazine feature, not a housepainter template — but stay grounded and handwerklich, never cold-luxury or corporate. Three words: warm, precise, grounded.

## Anti-references

Generic AI purple gradients, generic corporate blue palette, placeholder-only forms, glassmorphism, fake stock people/photos, agency meta-language. Also specific to this project: the reflexive uppercase eyebrow kicker repeated above every section, and side-stripe (colored left-border) card accents — both read as template scaffolding and undercut the "not a generic painter website" goal this brand explicitly set for itself.

## Design Principles

1. Prove skill through real material photography, not claims — every section earns its place by showing actual work (Kalkputz texture, Lasuren finish, real project rooms), never stock imagery.
2. Feel like a considered interior-design publication, not a housepainter template — one deliberate visual moment per section, not scattered decorative scaffolding.
3. Never obscure the phone number — direct local contact beats a lead-gen funnel for a craftsman business.
4. Preserve the original business's factual substance (Kirchstraße 37, 40227 Düsseldorf, 0162/9353172, real service list) while elevating its presentation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast throughout, including the dark plaster-toned sections where warm-brown-on-brown text is a real risk. Touch targets ≥44px. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Keyboard-navigable nav and forms with visible focus states.
